## Get Started in 3 Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your free account">
    Visit [churchos.faith](https://churchos.faith) and start your 30-day free trial — no credit card or setup fee required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import your congregation">
    Add members individually or bulk-import from a spreadsheet. Set up groups, custom fields, and your photo directory right away.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable giving and communication">
    Connect your payment processor, configure online or text-to-give links, and send your first announcement to the whole congregation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  ChurchOS is free to get started. Digital giving is also free — processing fees are handled by your payment processor (Stripe or PayPal). The Custom Church App add-on is available for $89/month.
</Note>
