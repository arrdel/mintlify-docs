Whether donors give through a web form, the mobile app, or a simple text message, ChurchOS unifies every contribution into one streamlined dashboard — making it easy for your team to stay on top of funds, run reports, and care for donors well. Over 500 churches rely on ChurchOS to power their generosity initiatives.

## The Giving Dashboard

The ChurchOS giving dashboard gives your finance team a real-time pulse on generosity across your church. At a glance, you can see:

- **Total monthly giving** — a running total of all contributions received in the current month, with a comparison to the previous month.
- **Donor count** — the number of unique donors who have given during any selected time period.
- **Fund breakdown** — a visual chart showing how contributions are distributed across your giving funds (e.g., General Fund, Building Fund, Missions).

You can filter the dashboard by date range, fund, or giving method to drill into the numbers that matter most to your leadership team.

## Processing Fees

ChurchOS is free to use for all digital giving features. Processing fees are charged directly by your connected payment processor — either **Stripe** or **PayPal** — according to their standard nonprofit or standard rates. ChurchOS does not add any additional platform fees on top of transactions. You can optionally enable a "cover the fees" option on your giving page so donors can absorb the processing cost themselves.

## Quick Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Your Payment Processor">
    Navigate to **Giving > Settings** and connect either Stripe or PayPal to your ChurchOS account. You'll need an active account with your chosen processor before completing this step.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your Giving Funds">
    Go to **Giving > Funds** and create the funds you want donors to designate their gifts toward — for example, General Fund, Building Fund, or Missions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share Your Giving Link">
    Copy your unique giving page URL from **Giving > Settings > Giving Link** and share it with your congregation via email, your website, or social media. You can also embed the giving widget directly on your ChurchOS website.
  </Step>
</Steps>
