Text-to-give removes every barrier between your congregation and generosity. Instead of opening an app or navigating to a website, a donor simply texts a keyword to your church's dedicated number, receives a secure giving link, and completes their donation in seconds. It's especially effective during in-person services, events, or any moment when giving on the spot feels natural. ChurchOS provisions your dedicated number and manages the entire experience on your behalf.

## How Text-to-Give Works

The text-to-give flow is straightforward for your donors:

1. **Donor texts a keyword** (e.g., `GIVE` or `TITHE`) to your church's dedicated phone number.
2. **ChurchOS replies instantly** with a personalized, secure link to your giving page.
3. **Donor completes the gift** by entering an amount, selecting a fund, and paying with a saved or new payment method.
4. **Donor receives a receipt** via text and email automatically after the transaction is processed.

After a donor saves their payment method the first time, future gifts require only a text — no link-following needed.

## Setting Up Text-to-Give

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Text-to-Give">
    Navigate to **Giving > Text-to-Give** and toggle on **Enable Text-to-Give**. ChurchOS will begin provisioning your dedicated phone number if you don't already have one assigned.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Your Dedicated Number">
    Once provisioning is complete (typically within a few minutes), your dedicated number will appear on the **Text-to-Give** settings page. This is the number you'll share with your congregation. You cannot change this number after it's assigned, so confirm it looks correct before proceeding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Keywords">
    Under **Keywords**, click **Add Keyword** and enter the word you want donors to text (e.g., `GIVE`, `TITHE`, `MISSIONS`). You can create multiple keywords and map each one to a specific giving fund — for example, texting `MISSIONS` could pre-select your Missions Fund on the giving page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test Your Setup">
    Before announcing text-to-give to your congregation, text one of your keywords to your dedicated number yourself. Verify that you receive the giving link promptly, that the link loads correctly, and that the correct fund is pre-selected if applicable.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Announcing Text-to-Give to Your Congregation

Once your setup is tested and ready, share the following information with your congregation so they can give on the spot:

- **Your dedicated phone number** — display it on screens, bulletins, and in your service flow.
- **Your primary keyword** (e.g., "Text GIVE to 555-123-4567") — keep it simple and memorable.
- Consider displaying the number and keyword on your presentation slides during the offering portion of your service, as well as in your weekly email newsletter.

## Member First-Time Experience

The first time a member uses text-to-give, they'll follow the link to complete their gift and save a payment method. ChurchOS securely stores their payment information (via your connected Stripe or PayPal account). On every subsequent gift, the donor can simply text the keyword and reply with an amount — their saved payment method is charged automatically, with no need to re-enter card details.

Donors can update or remove their saved payment method at any time by visiting your giving page and accessing their donor profile.

<Note>
  Standard SMS and data rates from a donor's mobile carrier may apply when they text your church's number. These are carrier charges unrelated to ChurchOS — there are no ChurchOS fees associated with sending or receiving text messages.
</Note>
