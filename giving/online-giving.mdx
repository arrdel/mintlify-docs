ChurchOS makes it simple to accept online donations through a fully customizable giving page that reflects your church's brand. Donors can give one-time or set up a recurring schedule, choose a fund, and receive an automatic receipt — all without leaving your church's giving experience. Once you've connected a payment processor and configured your funds, you're ready to start receiving gifts in minutes.

## Setting Up Online Giving

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect a Payment Processor">
    Navigate to **Giving > Settings** and click **Connect Payment Processor**. Choose either **Stripe** or **PayPal** and follow the prompts to authorize ChurchOS to process payments on your behalf. Make sure you have an active account with your chosen processor before starting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your Giving Funds">
    Go to **Giving > Funds** and click **Add Fund**. Give each fund a name (e.g., General Fund, Building Fund, Missions) and an optional description. Funds you create will appear as selectable options on your giving page so donors can designate their gift.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Your Giving Page">
    Head to **Giving > Settings > Giving Page** to personalize the donor experience. You can upload your church logo, choose brand colors, and write a custom thank-you message that donors see after completing their gift.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share or Embed Your Giving Link">
    Copy your unique giving page URL from **Giving > Settings > Giving Link**. Share it directly via email or social media, or use the **Embed Widget** option to add the giving form to any page on your ChurchOS website with a single snippet of code.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Giving Funds

You can add, edit, or archive funds at any time from **Giving > Funds**.

- **Add a fund** — Click **Add Fund**, enter a name and optional description, and save. The fund becomes immediately available on your giving page.
- **Edit a fund** — Click the fund name to update its label, description, or display order.
- **Archive a fund** — Click the **⋯** menu next to a fund and select **Archive**. Archived funds no longer appear on the giving page but retain all historical donation records. You can restore an archived fund at any time.

## Viewing Donation Reports

Navigate to **Giving > Reports** to access a full history of all donations received. Use the filter bar to narrow results by:

- **Date range** — view contributions for a specific day, month, quarter, or custom range.
- **Fund** — isolate gifts to a particular fund such as General or Building.
- **Donor** — search for a specific individual to see their complete giving history.

Reports can be exported as a CSV file for use in your accounting software or shared with your finance team.

## One-Time and Recurring Donations

Your giving page supports both one-time and recurring donations from the same form. When a donor visits your giving page, they can select their desired frequency — **One-Time**, **Weekly**, **Bi-Weekly**, or **Monthly** — before completing their gift. Both donation types are processed through your connected payment processor and appear together in **Giving > Reports**.

<Tip>
  If your church has a website built on ChurchOS, embed the giving widget directly on your "Give" page so donors never have to leave your site. Go to **Giving > Settings > Giving Link** and click **Get Embed Code** to copy the snippet.
</Tip>
