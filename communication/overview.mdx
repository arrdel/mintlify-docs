## Communication Channels

ChurchOS supports three outbound communication channels. Each channel has different reach characteristics and setup requirements — you can use them individually or in combination for maximum impact.

| Channel           | Typical Reach                          | Setup Required                        | Cost                              |
|-------------------|----------------------------------------|---------------------------------------|-----------------------------------|
| **Email**         | All members with an email address on file | Connect Mailjet or SendGrid account | Based on your email provider plan |
| **SMS**           | All members with a mobile number on file  | SMS credits included in ChurchOS plan | Consumed from your SMS credit balance |
| **Push Notification** | Members with the ChurchOS mobile app installed | None — enabled by default          | Included in all ChurchOS plans    |

<Note>
  High-volume email delivery requires connecting an external email provider. ChurchOS integrates with both **Mailjet** and **SendGrid**. Navigate to **Settings > Integrations > Email Provider** to connect your account before sending bulk email campaigns.
</Note>
