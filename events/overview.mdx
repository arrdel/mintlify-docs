From building a detailed order of worship to scheduling greeters and running children's ministry check-in, the Events module gives church administrators and ministry leaders the tools they need to keep every service running smoothly — without juggling spreadsheets or separate apps.

## What's in the Events module

The table below summarises every major capability available under **Dashboard → Events**.

| Feature | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Event Calendar** | Create and publish public or internal events, set locations, and manage RSVPs for your whole church community. |
| **Service Planning** | Build a detailed order of worship for each service, attach song lyrics and chord charts, and assign roles to individuals. |
| **Volunteer Scheduling** | Assign volunteers to roles for specific services, send confirmation requests, and automatically follow up with reminders. |
| **Check-In System** | Record attendance at services and events using kiosk, staff, or mobile modes, with label printing for children's ministry. |

## Getting to the Events module

You can reach the Events module at any time by navigating to **Dashboard → Events** from the left-hand sidebar. All four features — calendar, service planning, volunteers, and check-in — are accessible from the Events navigation menu.

<Tip>
  You can sync your ChurchOS event calendar directly with Google Calendar or Apple Calendar. Go to **Events → Calendar → Settings → Calendar Sync** and follow the one-click connection flow to keep your congregation's personal calendars up to date automatically.
</Tip>
