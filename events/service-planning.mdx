ChurchOS Service Planning gives worship leaders, pastors, and administrators a single workspace to craft every detail of an upcoming service. You can build a full order of worship, attach song lyrics and chord charts, assign responsibilities to specific team members, and share the finished plan with everyone involved — all without leaving ChurchOS.

## Creating a service plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Service Planning">
    Navigate to **Events → Service Planning** and click **New Plan** in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the service details">
    Enter the service **date**, **start time**, and a **service name** (for example, "Sunday Morning — 10:30 AM" or "Good Friday Service"). You can also select which campus or location this service belongs to if your church has multiple sites.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add items to the order of worship">
    Use the **Add Item** button to insert each element of your service in sequence. You can add songs, scripture readings, sermons, prayers, announcements, and custom items. Drag and drop rows to reorder them at any time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign people to each item">
    Click any item in the order of worship to open its detail panel. Use the **Assign** field to select the team member responsible — for example, your worship leader for a song, or your pastor for the sermon.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add notes or attachments">
    Within each item's detail panel you can type preparation notes and attach files such as sheet music, chord charts, slide decks, or sermon outlines. Attachments are visible to anyone the plan is shared with.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and share with your team">
    Click **Save Plan**, then select **Share** to send the completed plan to your team via email directly from ChurchOS. Recipients receive a link to a read-only view of the plan, including all items, assignments, and attachments.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Order of worship item types

When you add an item to a service plan, you choose from the following types. Each type includes a **Duration** field so ChurchOS can display the total running time of your service.

| Item type | Typical use |
|---|---|
| **Song** | Worship songs linked to your song library, with optional lyrics and chord chart display. |
| **Scripture Reading** | A Bible passage read aloud by a member, staff, or volunteer. |
| **Prayer** | Pastoral prayer, congregational prayer, or a moment of silence. |
| **Sermon** | The main message, with fields for title, speaker, and sermon notes or manuscript. |
| **Announcement** | Verbal or slide-based announcements from the front. |
| **Custom Item** | Any other element — offering, communion, baby dedication, and so on. |

## Sharing plans with volunteers and staff

Once a service plan is saved you can share it at any time by clicking the **Share** button at the top of the plan. ChurchOS sends an email to each recipient containing a direct link to the plan. Recipients do not need a ChurchOS login to view the read-only version. If you want to allow a team member to edit the plan, grant them **Editor** access from the Share dialog.

## Duplicating a previous service as a template

If your Sunday services follow a consistent format, you don't need to build each plan from scratch. Open any existing service plan and click **Duplicate**. ChurchOS copies the complete order of worship — all item types, durations, and assignments — into a new draft plan. Update the date, swap out the song titles, and you're ready to go in minutes.

<Tip>
  For churches with recurring service formats (traditional, contemporary, blended), save a clean duplicate as a **template** by naming it something like "Template — Sunday Traditional." Keep it in draft state and duplicate it each week rather than editing the master.
</Tip>

## Song library

ChurchOS includes a built-in song library shared across your entire church. To add a song, go to **Events → Service Planning → Song Library → Add Song**. For each song you can store:

- **Song title** and artist or composer
- **Key** and **tempo (BPM)**
- **Lyrics** (displayed full-screen during rehearsal or service)
- **Chord chart** (uploaded PDF or plain-text chords)
- **CCLI number** for licensing compliance

When you add a Song item to an order of worship, you search and select from your library. Any lyrics or charts attached to the library entry are automatically available to your worship team through the shared plan.
