## Setting Up Your App

Your app takes about 5 minutes to configure. Once you submit it, ChurchOS handles publishing to both app stores on your behalf.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings > Church App">
    In your ChurchOS dashboard, navigate to **Settings** and select **Church App** from the left-hand menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your app name and tagline">
    Type in the name you want displayed on the app stores and on your members' home screens (e.g., "Grace Community Church"). Add a short tagline that describes your church.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your church logo and pick brand colors">
    Upload a high-resolution version of your church logo (PNG with a transparent background works best). Then select your primary and secondary brand colors using the color picker or by entering hex codes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable or disable features">
    Toggle on or off the features you want available to your members — including giving, group chat, sermon streaming, prayer requests, and the member directory. You can adjust these at any time after launch.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview the app layout">
    Use the live preview panel to see how your app will look on a phone screen before you submit. Check that your logo, colors, and enabled features all appear as expected.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit for review">
    Click **Submit App** to send your app to the Apple App Store and Google Play for review. ChurchOS will notify you by email once your app is live and available for download.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The initial App Store and Google Play review process typically takes **3–7 business days**. Subsequent updates to your app (such as changing colors or toggling features) publish much faster, usually within 24–48 hours.
</Note>

## Sermon Streaming

You can deliver sermons to your congregation in two ways:

- **Upload recordings directly** — ChurchOS accepts MP4 (video) and MP3 (audio) files. After uploading, you can add a sermon title, series name, speaker name, date, and a short description. Uploaded sermons are stored in your ChurchOS sermon archive and appear automatically in the app.
- **Link an external video** — Paste a YouTube or Vimeo URL into the sermon entry to embed external content without uploading a file. This is ideal if you already publish sermons to a video platform.

For each sermon, you can also assign it to a **series** (e.g., "Advent 2024") and tag a **speaker** from your staff list, making it easy for members to browse past messages.

## Push Notifications

Push notifications let you reach every app user instantly — no email required.

To send a push notification:

1. Go to **Communications > Push Notifications** in your ChurchOS dashboard.
2. Enter a short notification title and message body (keep it concise — lock screen space is limited).
3. Choose your audience: **All Members**, a specific **Group**, or **Custom Segment**.
4. Select **Send Now** for immediate delivery or **Schedule** to pick a future date and time.
5. Click **Send Notification** to confirm.

<Tip>
  Use push notifications to alert members the moment a live stream begins, or to share urgent updates like service cancellations or emergency announcements. Keep the message short and action-oriented for the best open rates.
</Tip>

## App Store Listing

When your app goes live, it appears in both the **Apple App Store** and **Google Play Store** under your church's name. Members can search for your church by name and download the app just like any other app on their phone. The listing will display your church logo as the app icon and your tagline as the subtitle.

ChurchOS manages the developer accounts, certificates, and submission process — you do not need an Apple Developer account or Google Play Console account of your own.
