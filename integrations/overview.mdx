## How to Access Integrations

You can find every available integration in one place inside your ChurchOS dashboard:

1. Open the ChurchOS dashboard and navigate to **Settings**.
2. Select **Integrations** from the left sidebar.
3. Browse or search for the integration you want to connect.
4. Click the integration card to open its setup page and follow the on-screen steps.
