This guide walks you through the most important first steps in ChurchOS. By the end, you'll have a fully configured church profile, your first members added, digital giving enabled, and your first message sent to the congregation. The whole process takes less than 30 minutes, and you can complete it entirely within your free trial — no credit card required.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Head to [churchos.faith](https://churchos.faith) and click **Start Free Trial** on the homepage. Fill in your church name, your email address, and a secure password, then click **Create Account**. You'll receive a confirmation email — click the link inside to verify your address and activate your account.

    Your trial gives you full access to every ChurchOS feature for **30 days**, and no credit card is needed to sign up.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your church profile">
    After logging in, navigate to **Settings → Church Profile** in the left sidebar. Here you can fill in:

    - **Church name** — the name displayed throughout the platform and in member-facing communications
    - **Logo** — upload a PNG or JPG (recommended size: 400×400 px)
    - **Address** — used for check-in, event locations, and tax statements
    - **Timezone** — ensures event times and scheduled messages are accurate for your location
    - **Contact information** — phone number and website URL shown in emails and the mobile app

    Click **Save Changes** when you're done.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your first members">
    Navigate to **Members** in the left sidebar and choose how you'd like to add people:

    - **Manually** — click **Add Member**, fill in the profile fields (name, email, phone, address, groups), and click **Save**.
    - **Bulk import** — click **Import**, download the CSV template, fill it in with your existing data, and upload it. ChurchOS will map the columns and import all records at once.

    You can add unlimited member profiles on any plan, and custom fields let you track anything unique to your ministry.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable digital giving">
    Go to **Settings → Giving** and click **Connect Payment Processor**. Choose either **Stripe** or **PayPal**, then follow the prompts to link your church's account. Once connected:

    1. Toggle **Online Giving** to enabled.
    2. Optionally enable **Text-to-Give** and note the short code provided.
    3. Copy your unique **Giving Link** and share it on your website, in emails, or from the pulpit.

    Recurring giving and automatic year-end tax statements are available immediately after connecting a processor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send your first message">
    Navigate to **Communication** in the left sidebar and click **Compose**. Select your message type — **Email**, **SMS**, or **Push Notification** — then:

    1. Choose **All Members** as your audience (or filter by group).
    2. Write your subject line and message body.
    3. Preview the message, then click **Send Now** (or schedule it for later).

    ChurchOS tracks open rates, click-throughs, and delivery status for every message you send.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The ChurchOS mobile app lets you manage members, view giving, and send messages right from your phone. Search for **ChurchOS** on the App Store or Google Play to download it. If your plan includes the custom-branded app add-on, your congregation will see your church's own name and logo instead.
</Tip>
