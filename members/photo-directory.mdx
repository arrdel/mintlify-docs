The ChurchOS photo directory gives your congregation a friendly, visual way to learn names and faces across your church community. Members can browse a searchable grid of photos and profiles from the member portal or mobile app, and admins control exactly which details are visible to protect privacy. No more outdated printed booklets — your directory stays current as your congregation grows.

## How Members Submit Photos

Members can add or update their own photos without any admin involvement through two self-serve channels:

**Member portal:** After logging in to the ChurchOS member portal, members navigate to **My Profile > Profile Photo**, click **Upload Photo**, choose a file from their device, and save. The photo is sent for admin review before it appears in the directory (see admin management below).

**Mobile app:** In the ChurchOS mobile app, members tap their profile avatar, then **Change Photo**, and follow the same upload flow. Photo submissions from the app also enter the admin review queue.

## How Admins Upload and Manage Photos

Admins can upload photos on behalf of any member or approve submitted photos from the directory management panel.

1. Go to **Members > Photo Directory > Manage Photos**.
2. To upload a photo for a specific member, search for their name, click **Upload Photo**, and select an image file from your computer.
3. To review pending submissions, click the **Pending Review** tab. You can **Approve**, **Replace**, or **Reject** each submission. Rejected submissions trigger a notification to the member inviting them to resubmit.
4. To remove an existing photo, open the member's entry in the directory manager and click **Remove Photo**.

<Note>
  ChurchOS accepts photos in **JPG** and **PNG** formats. For the best display quality in the directory and printed PDF, upload images that are at least **400 × 400 pixels** and no larger than **10 MB** per file. Photos are automatically cropped to a square aspect ratio and compressed for web delivery.
</Note>

## Publishing and Sharing the Directory

By default, the photo directory is only visible to admins. When you are ready to make it available to your congregation, follow these steps:

1. Go to **Members > Photo Directory > Settings**.
2. Toggle **Directory Visibility** to **Congregation** (or **Leaders Only** if you want to limit access to group leaders and staff).
3. Click **Save**. The directory link becomes active in the member portal and mobile app immediately.

You can share a direct link to the directory by copying the URL shown under **Directory Settings > Share Link** and distributing it via email, bulletin, or any other channel.

## Privacy Settings

You decide which information is displayed on each directory card. Under **Members > Photo Directory > Settings > Privacy**, toggle each field on or off:

| Field | Default Visibility |
|---|---|
| **Photo** | Visible |
| **First & last name** | Visible |
| **Phone number** | Hidden |
| **Email address** | Hidden |
| **Home address** | Hidden |
| **Custom fields** | Hidden (configurable per field) |

Changes to privacy settings apply immediately across the portal, mobile app, and any future PDF exports. Members can also opt themselves out of the directory entirely by toggling **Include me in the directory** off in their own profile settings.

## Printing or Exporting as PDF

To produce a printed or shareable version of the directory:

1. Go to **Members > Photo Directory** and click **Export Directory**.
2. Choose your layout — **Grid** (multiple members per page) or **Full Page** (one member per page with larger photo).
3. Select which fields to include in the export (the same privacy rules apply, plus you can add or hide additional fields for print only).
4. Click **Generate PDF**. ChurchOS compiles the directory and makes the file available for download, typically within a few seconds for directories under 500 members.

The exported PDF reflects your current directory at the moment of export. Re-export any time you want an up-to-date printed edition.
