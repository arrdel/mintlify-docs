ChurchOS member management gives your team a single, organized hub for every person in your congregation. From first-time visitors to long-time members, you can store rich profiles, sort people into groups, track statuses, and keep your data clean — all without any per-seat limits.

## Navigating to the Members Module

To open member management, go to your ChurchOS **Dashboard** and select **Members** from the left-hand navigation sidebar. From there you can access all member records, groups, and directory settings in a single workspace.

## Key Capabilities

| Capability | Details |
|---|---|
| **Unlimited members** | Store as many profiles as your congregation requires — no per-record fees. |
| **Custom fields** | Add text, date, dropdown, and checkbox fields to capture church-specific data. |
| **Bulk import / export** | Upload member data from a CSV file or export your full list at any time. |
| **Family records** | Link individuals into family units for streamlined household communication. |
| **Member status** | Tag each person as Active, Inactive, Visitor, or Guest to keep your list accurate. |

<Tip>
  Use the **Filter** toolbar on the Members list to segment your congregation by status, group, campus, or any custom field — then trigger bulk actions like emails, group assignments, or exports directly from the filtered results.
</Tip>
